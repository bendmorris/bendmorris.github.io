When aliens invade Earth's moon base, only the janitorial robots stand between them and world conquest.

Program your robot's moves at the start of each round, and it will move at the same time as your enemies. Try to destroy all enemies in a limited number of moves.

Created for the GameBoy Jam in one week, in the style of a Game Boy game with a swappable four color palette.
